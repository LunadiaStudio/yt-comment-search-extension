Privacy Policy for YouTube Comment Search+

Last updated: June 21, 2025

YouTube Comment Search+ is a Chrome extension developed by LunadiaStudio to help users filter YouTube comments by content or author name. We value your privacy and want to be transparent about how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension does not use analytics, trackers, or any form of remote data logging.

  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.
  • No health or financial data is accessed or stored.
  • No communication content is monitored.
  • No authentication credentials are used or stored.

2. Permissions

This extension requires access to https://www.youtube.com/* in order to inject the search interface into the YouTube comment section. This permission is used only locally within your browser and never transmits any data externally.

3. Remote Code

This extension does not load or execute any remote code. All scripts are bundled and executed locally in the user's browser.

4. Third-party Services

This extension does not communicate with any external APIs or third-party services.

5. Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this privacy policy will be published at this location. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.
